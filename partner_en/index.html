The spread is 3.6% charged on every exchange — that's the platform's revenue. You earn your % of that 3.6%. Example: a $1,000 exchange → platform earns $36 → you at VIP (40%) receive $14.40. Formula: amount × 3.6% × your_%. VIP: $1,000 × 0.036 × 0.40 = $14.40 per transaction.
As long as your account is active, all clients you referred generate income for you. Lifetime means there's no time limit — the client is linked to your ID permanently, and every exchange they make triggers a commission. If they stop exchanging, no earnings — but the link remains.
